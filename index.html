If you are looking for exceptional sound,
there is no other choice.

As you already know, our hand-made acoustic drums and cymbals sound incredible.
Our electronic drums and cymbals sound incredible because we use hi quality audio samples recorded directly from our world-renowned acoustic drum kits and cymbals.

Combine the above fact with the fact that we engineer our electronic drums to behave as an acoustic drum. With over 1,000 dynamic levels, there is nothing else like it on the market.

Don't want your drums to sound like a standard acoustic drum? No problem. You will have over 700 premium quality sound effects to apply to your sound. You can even even create your own sound effects.

So, take your pick... acoustic or electronic? Either way, you will be getting the best sounding drums in the world. Just a heads up... electronic might be more portable for you.
